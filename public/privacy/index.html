Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Symply AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at symplyai.io and any of our AI tool products hosted on symplyai.io subdomains or dedicated domains.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, feature usage, timestamps, referral sources, and device/browser type collected via analytics tools.
  • Account data: Email address and authentication tokens when you create an account on any of our tools.
  • Payment data: Billing information processed securely by our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store your full card number.
  • Content data: Images, text prompts, and files you upload to our AI tools for processing. We do not use your content to train AI models.

2. Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Website analytics and usage measurement. Google may set cookies subject to your consent. Google Privacy Policy
  • Quantcast — Audience measurement for advertising platform verification. Quantcast Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Payment processing for paid plans and credits. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • fal.ai — AI model inference for image, video, and audio generation. Uploaded content is processed transiently and not retained by fal.ai. fal.ai Privacy Policy
  • Better Auth — Authentication framework for user accounts (self-hosted, data stays on our servers).
  • Cloudflare — CDN, DNS, DDoS protection, and static site hosting. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Application hosting and serverless function execution. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Neon — Managed PostgreSQL database hosting for user accounts and credits. Neon Privacy Policy

3. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. These are only set after you accept cookies via our consent banner.
  • Essential: Session cookies for authentication on tools that require sign-in.

You can manage cookie preferences via the consent banner shown on your first visit, or by clearing your browser cookies.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI tools
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. AI-generated outputs are stored temporarily for download and then purged. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default retention settings (14 months).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Contact us at adam@symplyai.io to exercise these rights.

7. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
